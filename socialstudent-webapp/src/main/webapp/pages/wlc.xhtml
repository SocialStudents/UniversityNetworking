  • Wear workout shorts, t-shirt and running shoes for weigh-ins. Please, there will be no exceptions
  • PBers are eligible to enter (and win!) BOTH Contests

  • Winner is determined by TOTAL BODY WEIGHT PERCENTAGE LOST. For example, a 200lb person who loses 10lbs has lost 5% while a 100lb person who loses 10lbs has lost 10%. The highest percentage lost wins!

  • “Like” our Perfect Balance page on Facebook!! We update daily with new information. We will post our workout videos and a meal plan to help you lose weight, get fit and be healthier!
  • Remember!!! Have fun and stay positive!!
